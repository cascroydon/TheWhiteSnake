The White Snake

Soon, you enter a forest where two ravens are throwing their young ones out of their nests. “You are big enough now,” say the ravens, “you can feed yourselves.”

The young birds lay on the ground and scream, “We will die from starvation because our wings are too small. We cannot fly yet and search for food.”

If you have some smoked fish, you can feed the ravens. Otherwise, you can kill your horse to feed the ravens, or leave the ravens and continue on your way.
