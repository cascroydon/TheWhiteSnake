The White Snake

You cut a piece of the white snake meat to taste it. As soon as it touches your lips you hear a strange voice say, “It looks very tasty.” Another voice answers, “Shall we get any!” You look around nervously, but you see nobody around except a couple of small birds perched on your window sill.

You return the plate to the kitchen and hope nobody noticed.

Later, you are summoned to see the king.
