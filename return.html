The White Snake

Later that day you are summoned to see the king.

“The queen has lost one of her most beautiful rings,” he says, “and you have been acting suspiciously. Find the thief by morning or you will be punished for the crime!”

You search the grounds for many hours without any luck, when you hear two ducks near the bank.

If you gained the ability to understand the language of animals, you can listen to the ducks, otherwise you continue searching.
