The White Snake

You catch the duck and take it to the cook.

The cook cuts off the duck's head and begins to clean it when he finds the queen's ring lying in its stomach. You take the ring back to the king who is astonished and happy. He is sorry that he doubted you and says, “Demand whatever position of royal honour you would like.”

Would you like a horse and money to travel the world, or a title and land within the kingdom?
